About Cap

A Place for Concept Art

This website is the ideal place for any graphic artist to post their work in order for it to be shared, commented on, apprecated by anthusists, building a strong portfolio for possible employment into the industry, and to join a loving community with people from similar talents. CAP offers a wide range of tools nessesary for you to get started. here are some of the people that have used CAP share their thoughts on how the website is ran.
